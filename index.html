Accelerate content creation in sports organizations

Mic'd Up is an innovative sports mobile platform that enables the creation of authentic live video shows, where fans voice their questions, AI moderated, resulting in an engaging, immersive experience

What we do

Protect Young Audiences

Many sports have a significant youth following. Content moderation helps shield young fans from exposure to inappropriate or harmful material, ensuring they can engage with their favorite sports safely.

Foster Healthy Discourse

Healthy debate and discussion are essential aspects of sports fandom. Moderation encourages constructive conversations and prevents discussions from descending into toxic arguments or trolling.

Uphold Brand Reputation

Sports organizations and sponsors have a vested interest in maintaining a positive image. Content moderation safeguards their brand reputation by preventing offensive or controversial content from associating with their name.

Enhance Fan Experience

A well-moderated sports community fosters a more enjoyable experience for fans. They can focus on their passion for the game, players, and teams, without distractions or negative interactions.
